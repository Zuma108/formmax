
# **Design System: Form Max Vitality**

## **1. Overview & Creative North Star**

**Creative North Star: "The Digital Curator"**
This design system rejects the clinical, crowded aesthetic often found in health tech. Instead, it embraces a minimal, high-end editorial feel that makes health data feel like a curated experience rather than a chore.

### **Design Philosophy**

* **Clarity over Complexity:** Prioritize clear information hierarchy and generous whitespace.
* **Human-Centric:** Use soft curves and a warm, dark-leaning palette to feel approachable yet professional.
* **Action-Oriented:** Use high-contrast interactive elements to guide the user toward key actions like inputting data or viewing insights.

---

## **2. Visual Foundation**

### **Color Palette**

* **Primary:** #1E1A24 (Rich Black / Deep Purple — used for backgrounds and primary text)
* **Secondary:** #FFFFFF (Pure White — used for cards, secondary backgrounds, and text on dark elements)
* **Accent:** #3B82F6 (Vibrant Blue — used for primary CTAs and highlights)
* **Background:** #F9FAFB (Subtle Gray / White — for light mode contexts)
* **Text (Primary):** #1E1A24
* **Text (Secondary):** #6B7280 (Muted Gray)

### **Typography**

* **Primary Font:** Inter (Sans-serif)

* **Headings:** Bold weight, tight tracking, #1E1A24

* **Body:** Regular/Medium weight, #6B7280 or #1E1A24

**Scale:**

* **H1:** 48px / 3rem
* **H2:** 32px / 2rem
* **H3:** 24px / 1.5rem
* **Body:** 16px / 1rem
* **Small:** 14px / 0.875rem

### **Shape & Surface**

* **Roundness:** 8px border radius for most UI elements
* **Shadows:** Soft, subtle shadows for card elevation
* **Borders:** Thin, light gray borders for container definition when not using shadows

---

## **3. Core Components**

### **Navigation**

* **Top App Bar:** Minimalist design with logo on the left and navigation links on the right. Transparent or solid white background.
* **Footer:** Simple links with app store badges.

### **Buttons**

* **Primary Button:** Accent Blue (#3B82F6), white text, 8px radius
* **Secondary Button:** White background, black text, black border, 8px radius
* **App Store Buttons:** Standard black pill-shaped buttons with white icons and text

### **Cards & Containers**

* **Information Cards:** White background, 8px radius, light shadow, generous internal padding (24px+)

---

## **4. Layout Principles**

* **Grid:** 12-column responsive grid for desktop
* **Spacing:** 4px / 8px baseline grid for consistent margins and padding
* **Whitespace:** Generous vertical spacing to create an editorial, premium feel

---

## **5. Interaction States**

* **Hover:** Subtle opacity change or slight elevation increase
* **Active / Focus:** Clear border or glow using the Accent color
