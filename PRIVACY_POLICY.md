### 2.3 Workout Videos — Important Notice

**We do not store, retain, or transmit Workout Videos to any third-party storage system.**

Workout Videos You record or upload are:

- Transmitted directly to our AI analysis service solely for the purpose of generating real-time Analysis Data
- Processed in memory on our servers and immediately discarded after the analysis is complete

- Never used to train AI models or improve our systems
- Never shared with any third party beyond the transient API call required to produce your results

Once your Analysis Data is returned to Your Device, the underlying Workout Video is permanently deleted from our systems. You retain full ownership and control of all video content recorded on Your Device.

## 6. Workout Video Data — Detailed Statement

Given the sensitive nature of video content depicting individuals engaged in physical activity, We are committed to the following binding practices:

1. **No storage.** Workout Videos are never written to disk, object storage, or any database by Form Max.
2. **No retention.** Videos exist only for the duration of the AI processing pipeline (typically 5–30 seconds) and are discarded immediately thereafter.
3. **No training.** Workout Videos are never used to train, fine-tune, or evaluate AI or machine learning models.
4. **No sharing.** Workout Videos are never shared with, sold to, or disclosed to any third party other than the transient API call to Google Gemini AI required to produce Your analysis results.
5. **No profiling.** We do not use video content to identify, profile, monitor, or track individuals.

The only output We retain from a Workout Video is the structured Analysis Data (scores and feedback text) that We return to You.
